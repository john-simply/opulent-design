Marine Sector

Our solutions for the marine sector

A carbon fibre lift cabin can weigh as little as 15kgs. This means it can easily be manoeuvred into position manually, with no need for the heavy machinery required to install a steel lift.

This provides designers and shipbuilders with greater flexibility in relation to the timing of its installation, whether as part of a new build or refit.

Our carbon fibre lifts allow owners of superyachts to incorporate the latest in innovation style by providing them with an exciting and exclusive, individually designed luxury product.
